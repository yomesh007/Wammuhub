About Tile Slide Puzzle

The Tile Slide Puzzle is a classic sliding puzzle game where the objective is to rearrange the tiles to form a complete sequence. In this game, you have to arrange numbered tiles in ascending order from 1 to n, where n is the size of the grid (e.g., 3x3, 4x4, or 5x5).

Objective: The goal is to move the tiles around the grid and place them in the correct order. The empty space allows you to slide tiles into it.

Gameplay:

  • Click on any tile adjacent to the empty space to move it.
  • The puzzle is solved when all tiles are in the correct order, starting from 1 and ending with the largest number (e.g., 1, 2, 3, ..., n).
  • Tiles can only be moved if they are adjacent to the empty space.
  • Try to solve the puzzle in the fewest moves and the least time!
