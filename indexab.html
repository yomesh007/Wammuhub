About the Games

1. Tile Slide Puzzle

  • Objective: Arrange the tiles in ascending order by sliding them into the empty space.

  • Modes:
    • 3x3: Beginner-friendly with 9 tiles.
    • 4x4: Medium difficulty with 16 tiles.
    • 5x5: Challenging mode with 25 tiles.

  • Features: Simple controls, reset and back buttons, and a move counter.

  • Why Play? Improve logic, focus, and enjoy solving fun puzzles.

2. Math Puzzle Game


  • Objective: Solve math problems involving addition (+), subtraction (-), and multiplication (*).

  • Gameplay:
    • Answer random math problems within a time limit.
    • Earn points for correct answers and improve your math skills.

  • Features: Multiple difficulty levels, interactive UI, and a score tracker.

  • Why Play? Enhance mental calculations and problem-solving abilities.

3. Tic-Tac-Toe


  • Objective: Be the first player to align three or more symbols (X or O) in a row, column, or diagonal.

  • Modes:
    • 3x3: The classic version of the game.
    • 4x4: A slightly bigger grid for more complexity.
    • 5x5: A challenging grid for advanced players.

  • Features: Multiplayer support, responsive design, and an interactive interface.

  • Why Play? Enjoy a timeless strategy game that sharpens logical thinking and decision-making skills.
