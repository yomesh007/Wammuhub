About the Game

This game is an engaging and interactive Tic-Tac-Toe experience that offers players the ability to compete on grids of various sizes, including 3x3, 4x4, and 5x5. Whether you're a casual player or a competitive gamer, this game challenges your strategy and quick thinking. With a simple interface and smooth gameplay, it provides endless fun for players of all ages.

The game is designed to be easy to learn but difficult to master. Players take turns marking their spots with "X" or "O" on the grid, aiming to align their marks in a row, column, or diagonal. The first player to do so wins the round. With each mode offering different grid sizes, the challenge increases as you move to larger boards.

Whether you're playing for fun or trying to beat your best score, this game offers a great way to pass the time and sharpen your strategic thinking.
