How to Play Tile Slide Puzzle

  • Step 1: Select a mode from the main menu (3x3, 4x4, or 5x5).
  • Step 2: Observe the grid. One tile space will always be empty.
  • Step 3: Click or swipe a tile adjacent to the empty space to move it.
  • Step 4: Keep sliding the tiles until they are arranged in ascending order (1, 2, 3... up to the last number).
  • Step 5: Complete the puzzle in the least number of moves and shortest time to achieve the best score.
  • Additional Features:
    • Use the Reset button to start the current game over.
    • Use the Back button to return to the main menu and select a new mode.
  • Tip: Plan your moves ahead to avoid unnecessary steps and complete the puzzle efficiently!
