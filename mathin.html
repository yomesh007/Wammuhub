How to Play Math Puzzle Game

  • Step 1: Select a difficulty level (Easy, Normal, or Hard) from the main menu.
  • Step 2: A math problem involving addition (+), subtraction (-), or multiplication (*) will appear on the screen.
  • Step 3: Either choose the correct answer from multiple options or type the solution in the input box (based on game settings).
  • Step 4: Submit your answer by clicking the "Submit" button or pressing Enter.
  • Step 5: Earn points for every correct answer and try to complete as many problems as possible within the given time.
  • Additional Features:
    • Use the Pause button to take a break during the game.
    • Check your score and track your progress on the scoreboard.
    • Restart the game at any time using the Reset button.
  • Tip: Focus on accuracy and speed to maximize your score and challenge yourself in higher difficulty levels!
