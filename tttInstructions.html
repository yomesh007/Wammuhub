Game Instructions

  • Objective: The goal of the game is to place your marks ("X" or "O") on the grid and align them in a row, column, or diagonal.
  • How to Play: Players take turns clicking on empty cells. The player who manages to align three marks in a row wins the round.
  • Grid Sizes: You can choose between three different grid sizes: 3x3, 4x4, and 5x5. The larger the grid, the more challenging the game becomes!
  • Winning Conditions: To win, place three of your marks in a straight line, either horizontally, vertically, or diagonally.
  • Game Reset: Press the "Reset Game" button to start a new game at any time.
  • Draw: If all cells are filled and no player has won, the game is declared a draw.
