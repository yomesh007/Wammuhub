About Math Puzzle Game

  • Objective: Solve mathematical puzzles by performing addition (+), subtraction (-), and multiplication (*) operations.

  • Gameplay:
    • Players are presented with random math equations or problems.
    • Select the correct answer from multiple choices or input the solution.
    • The faster and more accurately you solve, the higher your score.

  • Features:
    • Multiple difficulty levels: Easy, Normal, and Hard.
    • Timed challenges to test speed and accuracy.
    • Interactive and colorful interface to keep players engaged.
    • Score tracker to monitor progress.

  • Why Play?
    • Enhance math skills and mental calculations.
    • Boost focus and problem-solving abilities.
    • Fun and educational experience for all age groups.
