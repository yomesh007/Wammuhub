About Tile Slide Puzzle

  • Objective: Arrange the tiles in ascending order by sliding them into the empty space.

  • Modes:
    • 3x3 Mode: Beginner-friendly with 9 tiles.
    • 4x4 Mode: Medium difficulty with 16 tiles.
    • 5x5 Mode: Challenging mode with 25 tiles.

  • Features:
    • Simple and intuitive controls for easy tile sliding.
    • Minimalistic design for distraction-free gameplay.
    • Move counter and timer to track your progress.
    • Reset and Back buttons for convenience.

  • Why Play? Improve problem-solving skills, enhance focus, and enjoy a fun challenge suitable for all ages.
